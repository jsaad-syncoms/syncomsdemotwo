Our Process

Our multi-step process includes initial consultation and mockups before project commencement. We give clear and transparent deadlines for our work and outline the various phases of the project clearly. Our full-stack digital agency offers expertise in web2print, brand identity, website design, software development and digital marketing strategies so when you choose Syncoms, you can be sure that you're partnering with only the best.

Consultation

By identifying your goals and concerns, our developers discover your business's current people, process, and platform needs.

Planning

Mockups are made for you to visualise the end product. We start drawing our project roadmap from A to Z.

Execute

Kickoff! We start work on the project and our team of experts provide continuous support and updates on the status of deliverables.

Testing

The rigorous testing process begins! The project is uploaded onto a staging area where our QA team run all sorts of tests. You can even test it yourself as well.

Delivery

Final product delivered. Ongoing support is provided. Our account managers help you manage opportunities as you grow and your needs evolve. We're with you every step of the way.
